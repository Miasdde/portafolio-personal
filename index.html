Because They’re Family

BelPets is an integrated marketing communication project developed for a pet care and grooming brand based in Santa Lucía, Honduras. The campaign focused on strengthening brand awareness, improving digital communication, and creating emotional connections with pet owners through a warm, reliable and family-centered message.

• BRANDING PROJECT

REEFIUM

Reefium is a museum and gift shop brand identity created to promote marine conservation, environmental education and ocean awareness through a contemporary visual language.

The project combines organic forms, fluid movement and a carefully selected color palette inspired by the sea. The visual system was designed to communicate sustainability, accessibility and a strong connection between people and marine ecosystems.

The identity includes logo construction, typography selection, color strategy, editorial applications and branded merchandise, creating a cohesive experience across physical and digital touchpoints.
